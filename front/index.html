Senior Design Project

Team Astros

University of California, Irvine

Our project is a fully-functioning version of the Barca board game, which includes a strong AI engine as well as a modern user interface. Our team built this project from ground-up through designing a core game engine and embedding it as a web application.

Contributors to our project include Professor Pai Chou, who is provided the overall framework for the senior design course; Professor Sameer Singh who is our chosen mentor and person who delivered us guidance in artificial intelligence and machine learning; and Andrew Caldwell, the creator of the game, Barca.

This project will be a big contribution to the Barca community, as well as to our project development skills. We got familiarity with Artificial Intelligence and Web Development through our endeavors in making this game.
